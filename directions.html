General information about Azifa What is Azifa and Ingredents to Make Azifa.

Step by Step Directions To Make Azifa

  1. Place the lentils in a saucepan, cover with water, add 1 tea spoon oil and bring to a boil. Simmer lentils for about 25 minutes, until tender but not mushy.
  2. Mix in chopped chili peppers, onions, spices, lime juice, and oil.
  3. Add salt to taste.
  4. Refrigerate for a few hours to allow the flavors to marry, and serve cold.
